EVENTS AND ACTIVITIES

Board Games

  • Unwind from the day and gather around one of our many free-to-play board games. Whether you're with friends, coworkers, or study partners, it's the perfect way to relax, ideate, and spark friendly competition. From timeless classics to modern favorites, there's a game for every mood. Because sometimes the best memories are made over coffee and a good game.

Study Hour Upsize

  • Students deserve a little extra fuel, especially during long study hours. From 1-5 PM on weekdays, show your valid school ID and enjoy a FREE upsize on any drink. Bigger cups mean more focus, more energy, and more flavor to carry you through exams and group projects. Because studying is always easier with coffee in hand.

Loyalty Perks Card

  • Every cup you sip brings you closer to something free with our Loyalty Perks Card. Collect a stamp with each drink, and you 5th drink is on us. It's simple, rewarding, and the perfect excuse to keep coming back. After all, loyalty should taste as good as your coffee.

Pet-Friendly Sundays

  • Sundays are better when shared with your furry companions. Bring your pets and enjoy special discourses, while they get free treats of their own. It's a day for wagging tails, warm drinks, and happy vibes. Because coffee time feels even better with paws by your side.

Seasonal Specials

  • Keep your coffee experience with our Seasonal Specials. From refreshing blends for sunny days to cozy sips for the rainy season, and limited-edition flavors inspired by holidays and celebrations, there's always something new to enjoy. Each creation is crafted to match the mood of the moment—because every season deserves its own flavor.
