ABOUT US

At Pierre's Pressure, we believe coffee is more than a drink—it's an experience. Every cup is brewed with care, blending rich flavors and warm hospitality. Whether you're here to start your day, take a break, or share a moment with friends, our café is your cozy spot for comfort and connection.

SPOOK-TACULAR SIPS & SNACKS

Spooky season has arrived at our café! For a limited time, we're serving up frightfully fun drinks and hauntingly delicious treats crafted just for the occasion. Come sip, snack, and celebrate before they disappear into the night!

BLOODY VELVET LATTE

A rich red velvet latte crowned with whipped cream and “bloody” berry syrup. Served hot or iced, with chocolate bits “spider egg” for extra spook.

PUMPKIN SPICE COLD BREW

Cold brew topped with creamy pumpkin spice foam made from local calabaza squash and warm spices. A Filipino spin on a fall favorite, perfect for spooky season.

MONSTER MATCHA CHEESECAKE BAR

No-bake matcha cheesecake bars in monster green, layered on a buttery crust. Finished with Oreo “dirt” and candy bones and spiders for a frightfully fun bite.
